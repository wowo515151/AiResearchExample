Writer Secures $200M Series C to Drive the Frontier of Agentic Enterprise AI

San Francisco, CA — In a significant move for the generative AI sector, Writer, a leading full-stack generative AI platform for the enterprise, has announced it has raised $200 million in Series C venture funding. This latest round brings the company’s valuation to $1.9 billion, positioning the firm as a central player in the rapidly evolving landscape of enterprise-grade artificial intelligence.

The funding round was co-led by Premji Invest, Radical Ventures, and existing investor ICONIQ Growth. A diverse group of strategic and institutional investors participated in the round, including Adobe Ventures, B Capital, Citi Ventures, IBM Ventures, Salesforce Ventures, and Workday Ventures. Existing backers such as Accenture, Balderton, Insight Partners, and Vanguard also provided continued support. As part of the agreement, Sandesh Patnam, Managing Partner at Premji Invest, and Rob Toews, Partner at Radical Ventures, will join Writer’s board of directors.

A Full-Stack Approach to Enterprise AI

Since its founding in 2020, Writer has distinguished itself by moving beyond simple large language models (LLMs) to provide a comprehensive, "full-stack" generative AI architecture. Unlike general-purpose models, Writer’s platform is specifically engineered to meet the rigorous demands of the modern enterprise. This includes a suite of development tools, industry-leading graph-based Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and customizable AI guardrails designed to ensure security, privacy, and compliance.

The company’s technological prowess is headlined by its proprietary Palmyra family of LLMs. Most recently, the company released Palmyra X4, a frontier model that has achieved state-of-the-art accuracy in "tool calling"—a critical capability that allows AI to execute complex actions and workflows rather than just generating text.

Market Traction and Proven ROI

For investors, the most compelling aspect of Writer’s growth is its demonstrated ability to deliver tangible business value. The company reports that its customers see an average return on investment (ROI) of 9x, having saved millions of hours in productivity across various sectors.

Writer’s growing roster of blue-chip customers includes global leaders such as Mars, Qualcomm, Uber, Salesforce, and Vanguard, as well as major financial and service institutions like Ally Bank, Lennar, Prudential, and Intuit. This rapid adoption underscores a critical market need: the demand for AI that is not only intelligent but also reliable, secure, and integrated into existing business processes.

The Future: Agentic AI and Industry Expansion

The newly secured capital is earmarked for two primary strategic objectives: cementing Writer’s leadership in the enterprise generative AI category and accelerating the development of "agentic AI." While current LLMs are often used for simple task execution, Writer is focused on developing advanced, autonomous AI systems capable of planning and executing complex workflows across different systems and teams.

Furthermore, Writer plans to use the funds to rapidly expand its suite of quick-start AI applications and agents tailored for the most time-intensive workflows in three key sectors: healthcare, retail, and financial services.

As the industry shifts from passive chat interfaces to active, autonomous agents, Writer’s focus on "agentic" workflows and mission-critical reliability positions the company at the forefront of the next wave of digital transformation. With a dedicated team of approximately 400 employees and a robust roadmap, Writer is well-positioned to redefine the future of work for the world's largest organizations.
