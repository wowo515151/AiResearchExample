Together AI: Scaling the Open-Source Revolution through High-Performance AI Infrastructure

As the artificial intelligence landscape undergoes a decisive shift toward open-source models, Together AI has emerged as a critical infrastructure provider for the next generation of AI development. Founded in 2022 and headquartered in San Francisco, the company is positioning itself as the definitive "AI Acceleration Cloud," offering a comprehensive platform that spans the entire AI lifecycle—from training and fine-tuning to large-scale production inference.

Exceptional Growth and Financial Momentum

Together AI’s rapid ascent is backed by significant capital and a roster of elite institutional investors. The company recently announced a $305 million Series B funding round led by General Catalyst and co-led by Prosperity7. This round included participation from a distinguished group of global investors, including NVIDIA, Salesforce Ventures, Kleiner Perkins, Lux Capital, and Coatue, among others. To date, Together AI has raised a total of $1.54 billion, underscoring the massive market demand for high-performance, flexible AI compute.

The company’s growth is reflected in its extensive user base, which includes over 450,000 AI developers and AI-native companies. Global enterprises such as Salesforce, Zoom, SK Telecom, and The Washington Post have adopted Together AI to build and scale their modern AI applications.

A Research-Driven Competitive Advantage

What distinguishes Together AI from traditional hyperscalers is its deep-rooted commitment to systems research. The company’s research lab is led by Chief Scientist Tri Dao, the creator of FlashAttention. Together AI’s proprietary innovations, such as the Together Kernel Collection, have demonstrated the ability to deliver 24% faster training operations and significantly reduce costs for customers. Furthermore, their inference engine delivers 2–3x faster performance than current hyperscaler solutions.

The company’s research impact is felt throughout the industry; for instance, FlashAttention v2 is utilized by major players including OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, and Mistral. Additionally, Together AI’s release of the RedPajama-V2 dataset—a massive collection of 30 trillion tokens—has become a vital resource for the global AI development community.

Scaling Infrastructure for the Frontier Era

To meet the burgeoning needs of the AI ecosystem, Together AI is aggressively expanding its physical infrastructure. The company has secured 200 MW of power capacity and is deploying optimized clusters of NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs across multiple North American data centers. A strategic partnership with Hypertec to co-build a cluster of 36,000 NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 GPUs further cements its position as a leading AI Cloud provider.

The platform is specifically engineered to support the rapid evolution of open-source models like Meta’s Llama and DeepSeek-R1. By providing a secure, highly optimized environment, Together AI allows organizations to leverage these powerful models without the vendor lock-in associated with proprietary solutions.

Strategic Diversification and Market Position

Together AI’s ecosystem continues to expand through strategic acquisitions and partnerships. The recent acquisition of CodeSandbox has integrated built-in code interpretation capabilities into the platform, while a partnership with Cartesia has enabled ultra-low latency voice AI.

As the industry moves toward agentic workflows and more complex, multi-modal applications, Together AI provides the essential tools—including synthetic data generation, fine-tuning, and high-performance inference APIs—needed to move from prototype to production. For investors, Together AI represents a high-growth intersection of cutting-edge systems research and massive-scale infrastructure deployment, positioned at the very heart of the open-source AI movement.
