HiddenLayer: Securing the Next Era of the Intelligent Enterprise

As artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) transition from experimental technologies to mission-critical components of global enterprise operations, a new frontier of cybersecurity risk has emerged. HiddenLayer, a cybersecurity firm headquartered in Austin, Texas, has positioned itself at the forefront of this shift, providing the specialized infrastructure necessary to protect the algorithms, models, and data that power modern AI.

Addressing the AI Threat Landscape

Founded in 2022 by a team of security researchers—including those who identified the world’s first AI-specific malware—HiddenLayer was built on the principle that AI's success is contingent upon security. As organizations deploy autonomous, agentic, and generative AI systems, they face unprecedented vulnerabilities, including prompt injection, data leakage, and adversarial attacks designed to manipulate model outputs.

HiddenLayer provides a software-based platform designed to monitor the inputs and outputs of machine-learning algorithms. This allows enterprises to detect and respond to malicious activity in real-time without requiring prior knowledge of the specific AI model or the sensitive training data involved. By enabling businesses to secure AI without sacrificing speed or disrupting development workflows, HiddenLayer addresses the critical need for "security at the speed of innovation."

Comprehensive Product and Service Suite

HiddenLayer’s offerings cater to various stages of the AI lifecycle, from development to production. Their suite of solutions includes:

  • Model Scanning and Integrity: Detecting malicious models, backdoored weights, and vulnerable dependencies before deployment.
  • Real-Time Protection: Monitoring for prompt injection and unsafe AI behavior in production environments.
  • Adversarial Simulation: Using continuous simulations to uncover weaknesses and validate the integrity of the AI supply chain.
  • Professional Services: Providing threat modeling, ML risk assessment training, and red team assessment services to help organizations harden their defenses.

Financial Performance and Growth Trajectory

The company’s most recently disclosed valuation stands at $104.7 million. To support this growth, HiddenLayer has expanded its workforce to approximately 164 employees, comprising elite researchers and experienced cybersecurity executives.

By continuously publishing new CVEs, frameworks, and methodologies, the company does not merely react to threats but actively shapes the standards and defensive capabilities of the broader AI security community.

For investors looking toward the intersection of cybersecurity and the AI revolution, HiddenLayer represents a specialized infrastructure play designed to safeguard the foundational technologies of the modern economy.
