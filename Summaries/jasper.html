Jasper Therapeutics Advances Clinical Development of Briquilimab for Mast Cell-Driven Diseases

Jasper Therapeutics, Inc. (Nasdaq: JSPR), a clinical-stage biotechnology company based in Redwood City, California, is advancing its development of briquilimab, a novel antibody therapy designed to treat mast cell-driven diseases. The company’s therapeutic approach focuses on targeting c-Kit (CD117) to address chronic immunological and inflammatory conditions.

Recent clinical data has provided promising insights into the efficacy of briquilimab. In the BEACON study, 67% of additional patients (n=6) achieved a complete response at 12 weeks, demonstrating a mean UAS7 reduction of 31 points. Furthermore, in an open-label extension study involving 36 participants with chronic spontaneous urticaria (CSU), 75% of patients achieved either a complete response or well-controlled disease at the 12-week mark.

Briquilimab is a targeted anti-KIT monoclonal antibody designed to deplete mast cells. Jasper Therapeutics is currently evaluating the therapy across a range of indications, including chronic spontaneous urticaria (CSU), chronic inducible urticaria (CIndU), and allergic asthma. The company's primary mission is to develop safer and more effective therapies for patients suffering from these chronic, serious diseases.

The leadership at Jasper Therapeutics is comprised of experienced biopharma industry veterans with extensive track records in drug development and commercialization. The management team is led by Executive Chairperson Thomas Wiggans. With this experienced leadership, Jasper Therapeutics is well-positioned to achieve its vision of delivering new therapeutic options to patients with chronic mast cell-driven diseases.
