Fiddler AI: Driving the Future of Trustworthy Enterprise AI Observability

As enterprises rapidly integrate Large Language Models (LLMs) and machine learning (ML) into their core business processes, a critical gap has emerged: the need for transparency, security, and governance. Fiddler AI, a Palo Alto-based company founded in 2018, is positioning itself at the center of this transition. By providing an enterprise-grade AI platform designed to make AI services transparent, explainable, and understandable, Fiddler AI is addressing the complex challenges of deploying autonomous AI at scale.

Addressing the Complexity of the Agentic Lifecycle

The modern enterprise AI landscape requires more than just deployment; it requires oversight. Fiddler AI’s platform offers end-to-end visibility, context, and control across what the company describes as the "agentic lifecycle." This allows organizations to test, observe, protect, and govern AI applications to ensure they remain aligned with business goals and regulatory standards.

The platform’s technical capabilities are expansive, including data drift detection to maintain data integrity, real-time policy enforcement through rapid guardrails, and specialized methods for information security. Notably, Fiddler’s technology can generate explanations for machine learning models without requiring direct access to the model itself, providing a unique layer of security and interpretability for sensitive enterprise applications.

Proven Impact and Strategic Partnerships

Fiddler’s technology has demonstrated significant utility across high-stakes industries. In the defense sector, the U.S. Navy reportedly saw a 97% reduction in the time required to update ATR models through the use of such technologies. Beyond defense, the platform's applications extend to clinical care, patient outcomes, insurance underwriting, and autonomous financial operations.

The company’s market position is further bolstered by significant strategic partnerships. Fiddler has been named a tier-1 partner by AWS, featuring native integration with Amazon SageMaker AI. Additionally, the company maintains a close relationship with Capgemini, where Fiddler’s solutions are integrated into the Capgemini Reliable AI Solution Engineering (RAISE) to help industrialize custom Gen AI projects for global clients.
