Investment Profile: Snorkel AI

Overview

Founded in 2019 by a team of Stanford AI Lab alumni—including Alexander Ratner, Christopher Ré, Braden Hancock, Henry Ehrenberg, and Paroma Varma—Snorkel AI is a Redwood City, California-based enterprise AI company. Operating within the Business/Productivity Software industry, the company has positioned itself as a critical player in the development of modern artificial intelligence systems. Snorkel AI specializes in providing a Data Development Platform designed to help organizations programmatically develop high-quality datasets, which are essential for large language models (LLMs), retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, and AI agents.

Technological Value Proposition

The core of Snorkel AI’s value proposition lies in its ability to make AI data development a systematic and scalable software engineering discipline. The company utilizes an artificial intelligence-powered programmatic data labeling tool designed to extract information from complex text documents, such as electronic health records and scientific articles. By leveraging theoretically grounded techniques, Snorkel AI’s platform performs data augmentation and slices data into critical subsets. This allows enterprises to transform unstructured information into structured data resources, which is vital for specialized domains like bioinformatics and other highly technical sectors.
